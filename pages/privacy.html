Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

At Hardy, we believe in transparency and respect for your privacy. We collect only what we need to connect gardeners in your community, and we'll never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use Hardy, you may provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile photo
  • Location Information: Your address and neighborhood (only neighborhood shown publicly)
  • Listing Information: Descriptions and photos of produce, plants, or garden items you're sharing
  • Communication: Messages between you and other users
  • Payment Information: (Future feature) Payment details for Hardy Pro subscriptions or transaction fees

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use Hardy, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform (pages visited, features used)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • IP Address: To determine your general location and prevent fraud
  • Cookies and Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how people use Hardy

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Information Used
Connect you with nearby gardeners Location, listings, profile info
Facilitate communication Contact info, messages
Improve Hardy's features Usage data, feedback
Send important updates Email address
Process payments (future) Payment information
Ensure safety and prevent fraud IP address, usage patterns

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

To facilitate garden sharing, certain information is visible to other Hardy users:

  • Your display name and profile photo
  • Your general location (neighborhood, not exact address)
  • Your active listings
  • Your response time and activity status

Your exact address and contact information are NEVER publicly displayed. You choose when and with whom to share these details.

3.2 With Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who help us operate Hardy:

  • Hosting: Netlify (website hosting)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (usage statistics)
  • Database: Supabase (data storage)
  • Payments: Stripe (future - payment processing)
  • Email: Email service provider (future - transactional emails)

3.3 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to anyone
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your garden listings for any purpose other than connecting you with other gardeners

4. Your Location Information

How We Handle Location Data

Hardy is all about local connections. We use your location to show you nearby garden shares and connect you with neighbors. You control how specific your location appears to others - from just your city to your neighborhood. We'll never show your exact address publicly.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use Hardy
  • Improve our services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (ID: G-CJ4MS44MBB) to understand general usage patterns. This helps us improve Hardy for everyone. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects anonymized data about your visits.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Update: Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format

7. Data Security

We take security seriously and use industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted passwords
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal data

However, no online service is 100% secure. Please use a strong password and be cautious when meeting other users in person.

8. Children's Privacy

Hardy is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

As Hardy grows, we may update this Privacy Policy. We'll notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • In-app notification (when available)

10. International Users

Hardy is based in the United States. If you use Hardy from another country, your information may be transferred to and stored in the US.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

12. Future Features and Data Use

As we introduce new features like Hardy Pro subscriptions, marketplace functionality, and payment processing, we'll update this policy to reflect how we handle additional data. We'll always notify you of significant changes.

13. Contact Us

Questions About Privacy?

We're here to help! If you have questions or concerns about your privacy:

  • Visit our Contact Page
  • Email: privacy@hardyhome.app (coming soon)
  • Response time: Within 48 hours

Remember

Your trust is essential to building a thriving garden-sharing community. We're committed to being transparent about how we handle your information and giving you control over your privacy.
