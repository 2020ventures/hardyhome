About Hardy

Connecting gardeners and building sustainable communities

The community garden in your pocket

Hardy was created with a simple idea: to help home gardeners share their excess produce with others in their community. Too often, gardeners grow more than they can use, leading to waste. Meanwhile, others in the same neighborhood might love access to fresh, local produce.

We believe that by connecting gardeners through a simple platform, we can reduce food waste, build stronger communities, and help more people enjoy the benefits of fresh, locally grown food.

Whether you're an experienced gardener with extra harvest to share, or someone looking to enjoy fresh produce from your neighborhood, Hardy provides a simple way to connect.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing food waste and promoting sustainable gardening practices in our communities.

Community

We believe in the power of local connections to build stronger, more resilient neighborhoods.

Sharing

We celebrate the generosity of gardeners who share their harvest and knowledge with others.
